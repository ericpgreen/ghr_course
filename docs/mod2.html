Module 2

Consider Threats to Validity

By the end of this module, you should be able to:

  • Explain the main approaches to statistical inference and threats to statistical conclusion validity
  • Understand the fundamental challenge of causal inference, common causal inference methods, and threats to internal validity
  • Articulate why generalizability matters and the threats to external validity
  • Describe the importance of study measurement and ways to establish construct validity
